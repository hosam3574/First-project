What is physical therapy?

Physical therapy is the art and science of promoting health and preventing disease through understanding body movement. It corrects and mitigates the effects of disease and injury. Methods include assessment and treatment of patients, management and supervision of
physical therapy services and personnel, consultation with other health care systems, preparation of records and reports, participation in community planning, projects and future plans, and evaluation of educational programs.


What is the job of a physical therapist?

A physical therapist is someone with expertise in diagnosing physical disabilities, helping restore physical function and movement, and then maintaining these functions and body activity.
